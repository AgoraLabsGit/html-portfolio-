The Best Movies According to Alex

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Millers Corssing

A masterclass in neo-noir storytelling, Miller’s Crossing is a gripping, stylish gangster film with razor-sharp dialogue and unforgettable characters. The Coen Brothers blend intrigue, betrayal, and stunning cinematography into a tense, layered narrative. Gabriel Byrne delivers a cool yet complex performance, and the film’s poetic violence lingers long after the credits roll.

Dune

Denis Villeneuve’s Dune is a breathtaking sci-fi epic that fully immerses viewers in the world of Arrakis. Stunning visuals, a haunting score by Hans Zimmer, and a powerhouse cast bring Frank Herbert’s legendary novel to life with remarkable depth and scale. More than just a blockbuster, it’s a cinematic experience that blends spectacle with thought-provoking themes of power, destiny, and survival.

The City of God

A gripping, unflinching look at life in Rio de Janeiro’s favelas, City of God is both visually stunning and emotionally powerful. With its dynamic storytelling, raw performances, and kinetic energy, the film offers an unforgettable exploration of crime, ambition, and survival. Equal parts harrowing and exhilarating, it’s a modern masterpiece that stays with you long after the credits roll.
